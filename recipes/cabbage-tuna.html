Cabbage with Tuna

A very simple japanese side dish that is more than the sum of its parts.
Goes well as a side to meal with rice and can be eaten hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • 1x can of Tuna (personally I like the chilli tuna but any can will do)
  • 2x tsp of soy sauce
  • 1x tsp of seasame oil

Steps

  1. Chop cabbage
  2. Add the thicker stalk part of the cabbage to to pot of bioling water
  3. Wait about 30 seconds or so after adding the thicker bits of cabbage, then add the rest of the cabbage to the pot and cook for about 30 seconds.
    The idea is to have all the peices cooked evenly by staggering when theyre added to the water.
  4. Add cooked strained cabbage and Tuna to bowl and mix.
  5. Stir Soy sauce and Seasame oil through.

Notes

Optional: Garnish with roasted seasame seeds.
