Onion & Bonito Flakes

Another very simple japanese side dish that can be stored in the fridge for days.

Ingredients

  • 1x Onion
  • A handfull of Bonito Flakes
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Thinly slice onion and put slices into a bowl of water, let sit for 20 minutes
    This will remove some of the "spiceyness" of the raw onion
  2. Strain Onions, place in bowl with soy sauce and sprinkle with bonito flakes
