Peanut Doubanjiang Noodle Soup

A chinese style spicy noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • doubanjiang
  • Peanuts
  • sichuan pepper corns
  • dried red chillis
  • Celery
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • Dried Nodles
  • Stock

Steps

  1. (optional)Blanch diced meat to remove impurities. This will make for a clearer soup without scum/residue floating at the surface. Rince, drain and set aside.
  2. toast peanuts and dried red chillis on low for a couple minutes
  3. Add doubanjiang and stir for 30 seconds to infuse with the oil
  4. Add diced onions and saute. for 2mins
  5. Add carrots and celery, saute for another 3 minutes
  6. Add 1 cup of Stock and diced meat. cook till most of the liquid has evaporated
  7. Add another cup of stock and repeat the process
  8. In a different saucepan toast sichuan peppercorns until fragrant
  9. Grind or the sichuan peppercorns
  10. Add the sichuan peppercorns to the soup pot through a strainer. You dont want the husks and other large particles in the soup as this will give it a gritty texture.
  11. Add the remainder of the stock, bring to boil and then turn heat to low, simmer for 30minutes
  12. Add dried noodles and cook till noodles are done.
