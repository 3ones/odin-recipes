Thai Fish Cakes

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Optional: can of crab meat or prawns
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Coriander
  • Fresh Tumeric
  • Dill
  • Fresh Chillies
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • tsp Tumeric powder

Steps

  1. Poach or steam the fish until just cooked
  2. Finely chop all herbs and vegtables
  3. Whisk 1 egg
  4. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly

Notes

You want about 1 cup of meat to 1 cup of herbs and vegies to 1 cup of breadcrumbs and 1 egg.
