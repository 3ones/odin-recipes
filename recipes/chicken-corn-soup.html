Chicken Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • Corn cob x2
  • Chicken thigh x2
  • egg x1
  • Garlic x2
  • white pepper
  • chillis x2
  • 2 tsp corn starch
  • Chicken Stock cube x1
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp chinese cooking wine
  • Spring onion to garnish

Steps

  1. Steam corn cobs for 10-15mins then step aside.
  2. Poach half of the chicken till just cooked and set aside.
  3. Remove corn kernals from cob
  4. Add corn, pepper, chicken stock & Garlic to water that you poached the chicken in and simmer for 10mins.
  5. Blend the soup to your desired smoothness.
  6. mince the remaining uncooked chicken.
  7. in a small bowl mix the soy sauce, cooking wine and corn starch till there are no lumps and mix into the soup
  8. Add minced chicken and diced chillis to soup (slowly to avoid large chunks of chicken).
  9. Bring to boil to cook chicken then reduce temperature to low
  10. whisk egg in a bowl then slowly stir through the soup.
  11. Remove from heat and garnish with spring onions.

Notes

add salt and MSG to taste
