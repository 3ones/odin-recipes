Olive Pate Pasta

Ingredients

  • Olive Pate
  • 1 Onion
  • a few cloves of Garlic
  • 3 Dried Red Chillies
  • 2 Cups of Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Saute the Chillies and Onion
  2. Add the garlic and breadcumbs till golden brown
  3. Add Olive Pate with 1 cup of the water used to cook the pasta and stir
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, mix through and serve

Notes

Garnish with cheese and black pepper
