Bean paste Mince Meat

Ingredients

  • 500g Mince meat
  • Fermented bean paste 2-3 tbsps
  • Garlic
  • Chillis (dried and fresh)
  • Sichuan pepper
  • Soy Sauce 2 tsps
  • Chinese cooking wine (optional)

Steps

  1. Make chilli oil by frying chillis and Sichuan pepper in oil on low heat. This should take about 10 or so minutes, be careful not to burn the chillis. It's best to use a combination of fresh and dried chillis.
  2. Remove the chillis and Sichuan pepper from the oil and set aside
  3. add the minced meat and and garlic to the now chilli flavoured oil.
  4. Once the meat is no longer raw add the bean paste and soy sauce and mix through.
  5. Cook the meat for 10-15mins more. You can add a bit of cooking wine here if you wish.
  6. Add the fryed chillis back the mince and stir through.
